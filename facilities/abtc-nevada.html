Regional Significance

The ABTC Nevada Recycling Plant is strategically located in the Tahoe Reno Industrial Center, in proximity to Tesla's Gigafactory and other technology companies. This location provides logistical advantages for sourcing manufacturing scrap and supplying recycled materials back to battery manufacturers. The facility contributes to Nevada's growing clean energy economy and supports the development of a regional battery materials ecosystem in the western United States.
